Malignant Visions Motto:

Malignant Visions is dedicated to bringing high quality art and merchandise to the averae internet consumer. Our style is to provide eye-popping visuals that you may not see in the mainstream. We at Malignant Visions feel that every subject should be analyzed and leave no subject untouched. It's all okay, or none of it is okay. We really don't care if you don't like this philosophy either.

Future Additions to the Site:

In the future, we will be providing videos made by our studio, featuring our number one project, dubbed The American: Chronicles of the Fall of Man. We will take you through a journey of the world's destruction, showing how modern politics and religion could potentially shape the course of the future. Other endeavors that we will take up, include video games made for your PC and eventually, a forum for open blogging.

  • Artwork
